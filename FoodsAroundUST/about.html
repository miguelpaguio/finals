Foods Around UST

Foods Around UST aims to help the Thomasian Community to know the different foods that are being offered around the University and also, Foods Around UST helps the Thomasian Community know the different price ranges that are suited for their budget.

Aim of the Website

  • To help the students pick faster where to eat.
  • To know the price ranges of each different food stores.
  • To lessen the hassle of students that takes a lot of time of choosing a place to eat
  • To help the students know the other food stores and also helping the owner of the food stores.
